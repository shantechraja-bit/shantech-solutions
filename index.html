About Our Brands

Who we distribute for

Est. 1928 · Mitsubishi Materials Group · Japan

Moldino

Moldino Tool Engineering is a globally recognized cutting-tool manufacturer built around precision tooling for the die and mold industry. As part of the Mitsubishi Materials Group, it draws on group-wide research and manufacturing capability, supported by a worldwide network of sales offices, distributors and technical specialists serving automotive, aerospace, medical, electronics and precision engineering customers.

Its range spans milling cutters, end mills, drills, indexable tooling and high-precision systems built for high-speed, high-accuracy work — including machining hardened steels, stainless steels and aluminum alloys. Alongside its standard catalog, Moldino also engineers custom tooling for specific applications, continually investing in R&D to improve tool life, surface finish and overall machining efficiency.

100+ Years · Germany & USA

Guhring

Guhring has been a cutting-tool manufacturer for over a century, running more than 70 production and service centers worldwide backed by a large team of trained technical specialists. Guhring Inc., established in the United States in 1978, is headquartered in Brookfield, Wisconsin, with an additional manufacturing facility in Novi, Michigan for faster regional support — plus dedicated tool-reconditioning centers at both sites that restore worn tools to original spec.

The company stocks more than 50,000 standard products, one of the broadest ranges in the industry, spanning drilling, milling, threading, reaming and countersinking tools. Beyond the catalog, Guhring's engineering team builds custom tooling to customer drawings in HSS, carbide or PCD — often combining several operations into a single tool to cut cycle times and overall machining cost.

Est. 1937 · Japan

MST Corporation specializes in high-precision tool holding systems, built on decades of engineering focus on machining accuracy, productivity and reliability. Headquartered in Japan with rigorous quality control across manufacturing, MST's holders are used across automotive, aerospace, medical, mold & die and general engineering work, wherever repeatable accuracy matters.

The range covers shrink-fit holders, hydraulic chucks, collet chucks, face mill holders, milling chucks, angle heads and precision accessories — engineered for high rigidity, strong clamping force, minimal runout and longer tool life. Backed by a global distributor and technical-support network, MST continues to develop holding technologies that cut setup time and lift overall production quality.

65+ Years · Global

Master Fluid Solutions

Master Fluid Solutions has spent more than 65 years developing high-performance metalworking fluids, with a global manufacturing and distribution network serving automotive, aerospace, medical, energy and general manufacturing customers. Its focus stays on machining performance, tool life and operator safety, backed by ongoing research and hands-on technical support.

The portfolio covers semi-synthetic, synthetic and soluble cutting fluids, grinding fluids, stamping lubricants, parts cleaners, corrosion inhibitors and specialty industrial fluids — engineered for stronger cooling and lubrication, better corrosion protection, longer sump life and safer handling on the shop floor, all without giving up environmental performance.
